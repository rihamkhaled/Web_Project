2000 SQ/FT - 2 BR, 2.5 BTH

This remodeled Tudor cottage has maintained so much of its old-world charm, like the

original wood-burning fireplaces, exposed ceiling beams, and antique wood framed windows

with iron latches. Located in the coveted neighborhood of Briarwood Downs, the front yard

is filled with natural beauty, such as birch trees and flowering shrubs. The backyard is perfect

for grilling and gardening, and is big enough for an above-ground pool. The kitchen features

stainless steel appliances, quartz countertops, and vintage enamel coated cabinets. It leads

into a formal dining room with a built-in buffet and china cabinet. The master bedroom’s

ceiling is lined with restored crown molding and the master bath features a lovely clawfoot

tub. Throughout the house, the original walnut flooring has been preserved—a look that

takes you back in time.
